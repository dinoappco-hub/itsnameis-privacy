Privacy Policy — its name is.

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address for account creation and authentication. When you use the app, we collect photos you choose to upload, names you suggest, votes you cast, and comments you post.

2. Camera and Photo Access

Our app requests camera permission so you can take photos of objects to share with the community. Photos are only captured when you actively press the shutter button. We do not access your camera in the background. You may also select photos from your device gallery.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the app, display community content, send notifications about votes and comments on your submissions, and maintain community safety through moderation.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Photos are stored in secure cloud storage. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about activity on your submissions. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your submissions and account at any time. Upon account deletion, all associated data including photos, names, votes, and comments will be permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
