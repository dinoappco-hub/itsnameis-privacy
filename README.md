# itsnameis-privacy
## Camera and Photo Access

This app uses the **android.permission.CAMERA** permission to let you photograph real-world objects directly within the app.

- The camera activates **only** when you open the Snap tab — never in the background.
- Photos are captured only when you press the shutter button — never automatically.
- Photos you submit are uploaded to secure cloud storage and visible to other users.
- You can delete any submitted photo at any time from your profile.
- Camera access is optional — you can deny the permission and still use gallery upload.
- We also access your photo library, but **only** photos you explicitly select.
